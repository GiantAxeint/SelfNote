设空间曲线$\Gamma$为：
$$
\left\{  
             \begin{array}{**lr**}  
              x=x(t)          \\  
                y=y(t)
               \\
              z=z(t) 
             \end{array}  
\right.  
$$
则其在某点的切线向量为：$\vec{\gamma}=(x\text{'}(t_{0}),y\text{'}(t_{0}),z\text{'}(t_{0}))$

切线表达式为$\frac{x-x_{0}}{x'(t_{0})}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{y'(t_{0})}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{z'(t_{0})}$

法向面用线点式表示：$x'(t_{0})(x-x_{0})+y'(t_{0})(y-y_{0})+z'(t_{0})(z-z_{0})=0$
